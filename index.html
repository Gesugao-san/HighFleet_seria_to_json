HighFleet «.seria» to «.json» file converter

Give me your HighFleet «.seria» file and I will give you «.json» file. Or vice versa.
Expecting ship files from: "\HighFleet\Ships", but in theory may work with all HighFleet «.seria» files.
Duplicating keys will be numbered and values without keys will get it.
